## Introduction

In an era of information overload, we are dedicated to providing you with a pure and secure space for financial tracking. Piggy Bank eliminates cumbersome cloud synchronization and ad interruptions, returning to the essence of financial management: recording and planning. All data is strictly stored locally on your device, ensuring that every financial transaction is accurate and absolutely private.

## Key Features

- **Ultimate Security & Privacy**: Adopts a pure local file storage mechanism with no internet permissions required, completely eliminating the risk of data leakage. Your financial data remains entirely in your hands.
- **Financial-Grade High-Precision Calculation**: Built-in self-developed high-precision string calculation engine eliminates floating-point calculation errors from the bottom up, ensuring long-term accounting accuracy.
- **Multi-Target Management Mode**: Supports both "Cumulative" and "Target" modes for saving pots, intuitively displaying deposit progress, differences, and achievement percentages to help you efficiently reach your wealth goals.
- **Deep Interactive Experience**: A deeply optimized gesture operation system supports quick deposits and withdrawals via card swiping, custom shortcuts, and silky-smooth physical scrolling feedback.
- **Global Bill Perspective**: Provides a date-folded global transaction view with multi-dimensional financial statistics, giving you a clear overview of your income and expenses.
